About The Runer


"The Runer" optimizes your running experience by planning efficient routes. Just enter your desired distance, and get a route that's about half that distance.


Usage Instructions


• Enter Distance: Input your distance in kilometers.
• Get Route: The tool will generate a route that's roughly half the distance.
• Reach Location: You don't have to follow the exact path. Aim to reach the generated location.


Disclaimer


• The provided path is approximate. Actual distance may vary due to terrain and other factors.
• Use your judgment for a safe and suitable route.
• "The Runer" aids in planning, but you're responsible for your safety and route choice.
